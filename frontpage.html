BENGALURU CENTRAL STATE LIBRARY

BOOKS

A state library houses books across diverse genres, including literature, science, history, philosophy, and politics. It features classic English works by Charles Dickens and Jane Austen, as well as Kannada masterpieces by Kuvempu, Masti Venkatesha Iyengar, and Shivaram Karanth. The library includes poetry, folklore, biographies, and self-help books. Academic collections cover law, medicine, engineering, and environmental science. Kannada literature, including works by Da. Ra. Bendre and Chandrashekhara Kambara, is well-preserved. It also has encyclopedias, dictionaries, and rare manuscripts. The library serves students, researchers, and general readers, promoting education, culture, and intellectual enrichment across multiple disciplines.
